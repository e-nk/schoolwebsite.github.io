Message from the Director

First let me take this early opportunity to welcome you to Baraton College. Our college is a tertiary institution which offers several programmes ranging from certificate level to Diploma level. Baraton College is currently in the process of becoming a university through the proposed Great Rift University. This proposed expansion of our college has been necessitated by the current high demand for quality education in Kenya.As an institution we believe that good and proper education plays a great role in shaping our future and professional career. It helps us to develop personality and earn recognition and respect in the family and society. We can say that education is socially and personally an essential part of the human life. We cannot ignore the importance of education in our lives at any cost. Social issues like inequality, gender discrimination, religious differences and so many problems are there because of the lack of education in our lives. .

About Us

The Baraton College is a Centre of Excellence; with education related studies aimed at developing knowledgeable and skilled human resource for enhancement of transformation and productivity in the society.Baraton College was established in the year 2007 in Nandi County and admitted the first 5 students. The College is registered with the Ministry of Education to offer certificate, diploma and P1 programmes. The College started offering degree programmes in the year 2012 in collaboration with Gretsa University.
