About Us

The Baraton College is a Centre of Excellence; with education related studies aimed at developing knowledgeable and skilled human resource for enhancement of transformation and productivity in the society.Baraton College was established in the year 2007 in Nandi County and admitted the first 5 students. The College is registered with the Ministry of Education to offer certificate, diploma and P1 programmes. The College started offering degree programmes in the year 2012 in collaboration with Gretsa University.
